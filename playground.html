3D Playground

This is a short school project made to be an out-of-the-box 3D game.

The assignment was to show 5 previous projects in a creative way, and whatâ€™s better than making a whole playground for the user to explore?
3D playground shows in a fun and interactive way 5 projects that were made by me during the year. All the 5 projects represented an important value, such as: fun, growth, individuality, self-confidence and pride.
I tried to think out of the box and avoid the generic, embracing the "crazy"/funny side of me and making this project way more personal than I thought it would be. The result was a big playground with 5 different areas (one for each project), and a lot of small details that represent a bit of my progress through this year.
The playground can also be seen as a gallery/museum of my previous projects.
