    Beyond the Battlefield

    A visual novel about a veteran dealing with PTSD

    Embark on a profound journey with Beyond the Battlefield, a powerful visual novel that goes beyond conventional gaming experiences. Developed during my internship with No Worries Company, this project transcends the boundaries of entertainment – it's a poignant tool designed to raise awareness about the often-overlooked challenges of PTSD among veterans.

    In Beyond the Battlefield, each scar etched on the main character's psyche narrates a unique story, while every choice made shapes the destiny of a struggling veteran. This isn't just a game; it's an immersive exploration into the complexities of post-war life, offering players a glimpse into the harsh realities faced by those who have served.

    As you navigate through the narrative, you'll witness the impact of PTSD on the protagonist's life, relationships, and mental well-being. Beyond the Battlefield doesn't shy away from the uncomfortable truths – it confronts the stigma surrounding mental health head-on, fostering empathy and understanding.

    Experience the emotional depth and realism of Beyond the Battlefield, where storytelling transcends traditional gaming boundaries. Join us in shedding light on a critical issue, one choice at a time.

    Skills developed: Game design, C#, researching, Visual Novel.

    Game Design Document

    Nightflame: Ascension of Nightmares

    Undertaking a solo endeavor, I was tasked with creating a comprehensive game design document in response to a client's specific request. The assignment revolved around conceptualizing a hero for a new franchise, drawing inspiration from the iconic Marvel Universe, and subsequently developing a game centered around this protagonist.

    The hero's primary ability, akin to Iron Man's prowess, involved the unique capability of shooting. Tailoring the game to cater to a hardcore gaming audience, the vision was to create an experience reminiscent of titles like Batman: Arkham Asylum, infused with an additional layer of innovation.

    Night Flame: Ascension of Nightmares unfolds as a suspenseful and immersive gaming experience, boasting distinctive mechanics that set it apart. A key selling point is its utilization of motion control, where the main character mirrors the exact movements executed with the controllers.
