Game Design Document

The game I designed at school.

The assignment was to make a game design document all by yourself, following the request of a client.
The request was basically to make a hero for a new franchise (something inspired by the marvel universe) and make a game around it.
The hero's main ability was that he could shoot just like iron man. The game's target audience is hardcore gamers, so why not make it something like Batman Arkhan Asylum? But, of course, with some extra spice.
Night Flame: Ascension of Nightmares is full of suspense and unique mechanics. It's unique selling point? Motion control. The main character follows exactly the movements you do with the controllers.
