Canastra score (Pontos da Canastra)

The score app made for you to never worry about having pen and paper around when playing!

Pontos da Canastra is a score website specially made for people that play the cards game named "Canastra/Buraco".
On this website you can add up to 2 teams, name them, constantly add new scores to each team and know when a team wins. The score is stored and shown on the screen, every input is added to the last one (making the total score).
