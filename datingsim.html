Sint Lucas Dating Simulator

A casual dating simulator made in Renpy.

With the help of a very nice group of designers and another programmer, Sint Lucas Dating Sim was made to bring joy to everyone.
Working on it was really fun, and I Iearned a lot from it as well. I've never programmed in Renpy before, so it was a big step to do this project. I'm really glad I had enthusiastic people by my side, working hard to make this project come true.
Sint Lucas dating sim is a game that focuses on having lots of possibilities for the player to interact with whoever they want.
